The Solution: US SIM Cards & VPNs
Do not use free VPNs

Platforms like TikTok and Instagram easily detect cheap VPNs and will shadowban your account instantly.

Best Method: US SIM Card

Buy a cheap, prepaid physical US SIM card (like Mint Mobile) from eBay or Amazon. Put it in an old, factory-reset phone. Do not connect to local Wi-Fi; use a high-quality Residential Proxy. The platforms will read the SIM card and push your content to the US feed.

Secondary Method: Residential VPN

If you cannot get a SIM, purchase a dedicated "Residential IP" VPN. This makes your internet connection look like a real house in the US, rather than a massive data center.

Chapter 02 3-Day Warm-Up
Mandatory Protocol

This applies to both TikTok and Instagram Reels. Both platforms flag new accounts as bots if you skip the warmup. Skip it = shadowban. Follow it = unlock real views.

Account Setup (Before Day 1)
Complete profile 100% — real photo, bio filled out, niche-relevant username
Enable two-factor authentication, use a valid email
NO links in bio yet — add them after warmup is complete
Post from the mobile app only for the first 10+ posts
The 3-Day Timeline
1
Day 1
Pure Scroll
  • Spend 30+ min on FYP / Reels feed
  • Watch videos all the way through, don't skip in patterns
  • Like 15–20 videos in your niche, spread out
  • Leave 5–7 genuine comments
  • Follow 8–10 accounts in your niche. No posting.
2
Day 2
Train the Algorithm
  • Repeat Day 1 engagement
  • Search niche keywords directly
  • Watch 10+ niche videos all the way through
  • Engage with a few non-niche videos too. Still no posting.
3
Day 3
Drop ONE Warm-Up Post
  • Niche content only — not promotional
  • Use a trending sound from the last 7 days
  • 1K+ views = cleared to post freely
  • Under 200 views = account is cooked, start over
Auto-Flags — Never Do These
  • Posting within 24 hours of account creation
  • Putting links in your bio on Day 1
  • Mass following or unfollowing accounts
  • Posting multiple times before getting any engagement
  • Comments that are just emojis (must use genuine words)
Chapter 03 How to Post
Step 1: Edit for Retention
  • The Hook: Ensure the first 0–3 seconds feature bold visuals or text to stop the scroll
  • Quality: Use high-definition footage — avoid watermarks from other apps
  • Captions: Use tight, bold, clean text overlays for accessibility
Step 2: The 15-Minute Rule ⏱

Once you post your video on social media, you must immediately go to your Clicksable HQ Dashboard. You have exactly 15 minutes to submit the public URL link of your video into the active campaign.

If you submit after 15 minutes, your video will be automatically rejected.

Quick Post Checklist
Hook is visible in the first 3 seconds
No watermarks from CapCut or other editing apps
Video is in HD quality
Captions / text overlays added
Timer starts now — submit URL in HQ within 15 minutes
